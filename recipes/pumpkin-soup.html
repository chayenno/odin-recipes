Pumpkin Soup

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.

Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 2â€‰1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 chopped onion
  1. Heat chicken broth, pumpkin puree, onion, garlic, and Cajun seasoning to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 45 to 60 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in heavy cream before serving.
